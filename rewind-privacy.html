Privacy Policy

Rewind App
Effective Date: January 12, 2026
Last Updated: January 12, 2026

Privacy-First Architecture: Your photos NEVER leave your device. All photo processing happens locally on your phone. We don't upload, store, or access your actual photos on any server. Period.

1. Introduction

Amer Kumar, operating as HiredAI Labs ("we," "our," or "us"), develops and operates the Rewind mobile application (the "App"). Rewind is a photo cleanup app that helps users organize their photo library by showing memories from today's date in previous years. Users swipe to keep or archive photos.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App on iOS and Android platforms. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws worldwide.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

Developer Information:

  • Developer: Amer Kumar
  • Business Name: HiredAI Labs
  • Contact Email: tech@hiredai.ca
  • Platforms: iOS and Android
  • Availability: Worldwide (all countries)

2. Our Privacy-First Approach

What This Means for You:

  • ALL photo processing happens locally on your device
  • Photos NEVER leave your device or get uploaded to any server
  • When you archive a photo, it goes to your device's "Recently Deleted" folder (30-day recovery on iOS; recovery period varies on Android)
  • We cannot see, access, or retrieve your photos

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Optional Account Data

You can use Rewind without creating an account. If you choose to create an account for streak backup and sync, we collect:

Data Type Purpose Required?
Phone Number Account authentication via Firebase Phone Auth, streak backup/sync Required for account
Name Profile personalization Optional
Email Address Weekly progress emails (opt-in), account communications Optional

3.2 App Usage Data (Stored Locally on Device)

The following data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you create an account:

  • Swipe Statistics: Photos kept, photos archived, storage space saved
  • Streak Progress: Consecutive days of app use
  • App Preferences: Settings and customization choices

3.3 Cloud-Synced Data (Only if Phone Verified)

If you verify your phone number and create an account, the following data is synced to our cloud servers:

  • Streak Data: Your cleanup streak for backup and cross-device sync
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version
  • Aggregated Cleanup Statistics: Total photos reviewed, storage reclaimed

3.4 Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. This includes:

  • Anonymous usage patterns (features used, screens viewed)
  • App performance metrics
  • Crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally.

3.5 Photo Library Access

Critical Privacy Protection: We request access to your photo library solely to display photos within the App so you can review them. Your photos are NEVER uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any external server. All photo processing happens entirely on your device.

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

To be clear about our privacy practices:

  • We NEVER access, upload, or store your actual photos
  • We do NOT sell any personal data to third parties
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We do not collect precise location data
  • We do not collect contacts or address book information
  • We do not collect health or financial information

5. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
  • Sync your progress (streaks, statistics) across devices when you sign in
  • Send you optional weekly progress emails (only if you opt in and provide email)
  • Analyze anonymous usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

6.1 Firebase (Google)

Firebase provides several services for our App:

  • Firebase Phone Authentication: Secure phone number verification for account creation
  • Cloud Firestore: Database for storing streak and account data
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting and app stability monitoring
  • Firebase Remote Config: App configuration management
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notifications (if enabled)

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

6.2 Google AdMob

For users of the free version, we display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • Device information
  • General location (country/region level)

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. Premium users do not see advertisements.

6.3 Apple StoreKit (iOS)

In-app purchases on iOS are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment information, credit card details, or billing address. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

6.4 Google Play Billing (Android)

In-app purchases on Android are processed entirely by Google through Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment information, credit card details, or billing address. See Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Storage and Security

7.1 Where Data is Stored

Data Type Storage Location
Your Photos Your device only (NEVER uploaded)
Local app data (stats, preferences) Your device only
Account data (if signed up) Firebase/Google Cloud servers
Analytics data Firebase/Google Analytics servers

7.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of data at rest on cloud servers
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Phone Auth
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained on your device until you delete the App
  • Cloud Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 26 months, then automatically deleted
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 90 days

You can request deletion of your account and all associated cloud data at any time.

9. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

9.1 All Users

  • Delete Account: Delete your account and all associated data through the App settings or by visiting our Delete Account page
  • Export Data: Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Opt Out of Analytics: Disable analytics tracking in the App settings
  • Control Permissions: Manage app permissions (photos, notifications) in your device settings

9.2 Managing Device Permissions

iOS:

  • Photo Library: Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → Rewind
  • Notifications: Settings → Notifications → Rewind

Android:

  • Photo Library: Settings → Apps → Rewind → Permissions
  • Notifications: Settings → Apps → Rewind → Notifications

Note: Revoking photo library access will prevent the App from functioning as intended.
